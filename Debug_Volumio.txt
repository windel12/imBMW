COMMON COMMANDS:

ls /dev/tty*
stty -F /dev/ttyUSB0 -cstopb cs8 -parenb 9600
		-cstopb is 1 stop bit
		-cs8 is 8 bits
		-parenb is no parity
		9600 is the baud rate
